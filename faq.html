Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ! Here you'll find answers to common questions about wave, our privacy practices, and tips for the best listening experience. Whether you're looking for a specific station or just want to tune in, wave makes it easy.

What is Wave? expand_more

Wave is a web-based player for publicly available Philippine radio streams. We gather live broadcasts from across the country, allowing you to listen to your favorite music, news, and talk shows online.

Is listening to radio on Wave legal? expand_more

Yes. We only provide links to publicly accessible streams and do not host any content ourselves. Every stream is believed to be provided by its legal copyright holder for public distribution. You can find more details in the Legal Disclaimer on our "About Us" page.

Why are some stations unavailable or not working? expand_more

A station might be temporarily down due to maintenance on their end or network issues. We check our streams for quality, but since we don't control the broadcasts, some downtime is occasionally unavoidable. Please try again later.

How does Wave protect my privacy? expand_more

Your privacy is our top priority. We do not require you to sign up, and we do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. You can listen with peace of mind knowing your information remains completely private.
