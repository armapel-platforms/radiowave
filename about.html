About Us

About wave

Wave is a free-to-listen Philippine radio hub designed to bring the vibrant sounds of the Philippines to listeners everywhere. Our mission is to make local radio accessible to everyone, celebrating Filipino culture, music, and news. From the latest OPM hits to talk radio, Wave offers a direct connection to the heart of Filipino broadcasting, all from the comfort of your browser.

Our Mission

At Wave, we believe in a simple, reliable, and seamless listening experience. We aim to connect you with high-quality audio streams without the clutter of broken links or invasive ads. We're here to spare you the hassle of endless searching, so you can simply tune in and enjoy the sounds of home.

Technology Behind the Scenes

Wave is built with powerful, modern web technologies. Our audio playback is powered by Shaka Player for a robust and reliable streaming experience. The user interface icons are provided by Google Fonts (Material Symbols), ensuring a clean and intuitive design.

Legal Disclaimer

Wave provides access to publicly available radio streams but does not host, own, or control any audio content. We serve as a directory to help you discover and listen to streams that we believe have been authorized for public distribution by the copyright holders. We do not guarantee the legality or quality of external content.
